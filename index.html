The process

  • delivery

An important part of the process is capturing the footage. We will sit down with you before the shoot and make sure we have clear understanding of what you want to see, once the shot list has been established we can start pulling the process together.

We want to know what your vision is and how you want the finished product to look, but if you’re not sure what you want – even better because we love to create! Its also important to note that we can shoot in may different formats and styles depending on your taste or flare. We also understand that everybody has a budget, so with that in mind we will work to customize a package that suits your needs and is within your budget.

our packages

vidoe & photography

We offer full video and photography packages for weddings, corporate events and special events. Whatever you can think of – we can do!

At Studio66 we feel that everyone’s needs are different as no two weddings or events are the same. So with that in mind we prefer to sit down with you and discuss your vision.

There are a lot of different factors we take into consideration before a price is quoted. We offer very competitive pricing and understand all too well the cost overruns associated with a wedding. We are sure you will be pleasantly surprised and extremely happy with the end result.

Our goal is to ensure that you get what you want while staying within your budget. We love to do things differently and have an “out of the box” thought process. We are sure you want your special occasion to be different from all the others.

Studio66 provides live feed video services as well.

Give us a call to discuss your needs and we will cater a customized package for your special day!

get to know us

Unrivalled creativity and fresh thinking is what sets us apart.

Every time we pick up the camera we strive to create something special and unique…In order to be good at something you need to have passion and drive, otherwise you just – well – go through the motions.

When it comes to using a camera we are very passionate and dedicated to the principals and the dynamics of this incredible way of capturing moments in time. Understanding what’s on the other side of the lens is paramount in the process of creating something special. Using this approach enables us to create a very unique perspective utilizing our style and your interpretation of whatever we are shooting to produce the best possible results and of course some surprises! We would love the opportunity to be able to work with you and transform what you envision, into reality!
